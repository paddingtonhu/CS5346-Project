Why Bilibili?

Bilibili is a holding company that owns an iconic brand that represents the online entertainment of China's younger generation. It creates a new viewing experience, allowing users to benefit from a strong emotional bond with other users, sharing similar desires and interests. Due to the richness of its content, it has been welcomed by people from all ages, especially Generation Z (individuals born from 1990 to 2009) in China.

Content

Originally a content community inspired by animation, manga and games or ACG. Bilibili has evolved into a full-spectrum online entertainment world covering a variety of genres and media formats (including video, live streaming and mobile games.

Bullet chat

Bilibili's unique design feature is its interactive function, the "bullet chat" function, through the real-time comment function, viewers share their thoughts and feelings of watching the same video.

Query

1.Does the playback volume affect the number of bullet chats?

2.What words are popular among audience, and how is their sentiment?

3.What are the characteristics of the most popular video uploaders in the movie, life and game categories? How do their videos perform?
